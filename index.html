Soccer Skills World Cup is a fast-paced 3D soccer game where you lead your favorite national team to victory.

Play against the computer or real players in tournaments, progressing from quarter-finals to the championship.

Drag your finger or mouse to control movement and kicks, with speed and power determined by your drag intensity.

Experience throw-ins, corner kicks, fouls, and penalties. Whether you call it soccer or football, this game will test your skills and put you right in the action!
